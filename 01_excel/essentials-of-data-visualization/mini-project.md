## Objective
To create an interactive Excel dashboard that provides insights into sales performance and supports data-driven decision-making.

## Dataset
Sample sales dataset containing:
- Date
- Region
- Product
- Quantity Sold
- Revenue

## Excel Features Used
- Pivot Tables
- Column Charts
- Line Charts
- Conditional Formatting
- Filters and Slicers

## Visualizations Created
1. Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
2. Revenue by Region (Column Chart)
3. Product-wise Sales Distribution (Bar Chart)
4. KPI Metrics:
   - Total Revenue
   - Total Units Sold
   - Best Performing Region

## Insights Derived
- Sales peak during mid-year months.
- One region contributes more than 40% of total revenue.
- A small number of products generate majority of sales.
- Seasonal patterns affect overall performance.

## Outcome
This dashboard enables quick understanding of sales trends and helps management make informed decisions without reviewing raw data.

## Possible Improvements
- Automate data refresh
- Add forecast analysis
- Improve interactivity with advanced slicers
